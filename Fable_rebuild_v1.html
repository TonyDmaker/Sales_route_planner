Help
  • Long-press the map to drop a status pin or park the car. Right-click works on desktop.
  • Tap a pin to write notes, change its status, move it, or delete it.
  • Draw a perimeter around the neighborhood, then Generate route — it covers both sides of every street and returns to the car.
  • The ● button starts tracking: your trail is recorded and streets turn green as you walk them. The chip up top shows distance and coverage.
  • GPS only works over HTTPS. Easiest hosting: drag this file onto app.netlify.com/drop (free) and open the link it gives you on your phone.
  • Everything saves on this device automatically. Use Backup JSON to move a day to another device.
